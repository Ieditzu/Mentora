# Paper Summary: MDPI 2025 Meta-Analysis
**Title:** "Effectiveness of Adaptive AI Tutoring in K-12 Programming Education"
**Source:** MDPI Education Sciences (2025)

### Key Findings:
1. **Retention:** Adaptive AI tutoring leads to a **58% improvement in knowledge retention** over six months compared to static online courses.
2. **Performance:** Students in adaptive groups outscored control groups by an average of **0.86 standard deviations** (SMD) on final logic assessments.
3. **Confidence:** AI's "non-judgmental" environment significantly reduced learning anxiety in novice coders.

### Why this matters for Mentora:
Mentora's persistent "Learning Fingerprint" (JSONB profile) is the engine for this retention boost. It ensures the AI adapts based on a student's long-term history, not just their current task.
